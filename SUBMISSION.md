# Kredito — sponsor submission

**Kredito** brings real-world creditworthiness onchain so small, fast-growing emerging-market
businesses can borrow **undercollateralized** USDC at competitive rates. A business proves its
financial health through **private** document analysis, receives an **issuer-signed onchain credit
identity**, and can request funding against it — all gasless, with no wallet setup.

**Live:** _<set to the production URL once `feat/working-demo` is deployed>_
**Chain:** Ethereum **Sepolia** (11155111)
**Repo:** https://github.com/Ekinoxis-evm/thelender

**The flow:** Login (Privy, gasless) → upload business docs → **Confidential AI credit score** (Chainlink TEE)
→ mint **`<label>.kredito.eth`** credit identity (ENS) → customize profile → request funding.

---

## 🔗 Chainlink — Confidential AI Attester

The credit score is **100% generated by the Chainlink Confidential AI Attester**: a business's
audited financial documents are analyzed inside an **AWS Nitro TEE** (raw documents never leave the
enclave or touch our database). The pipeline runs **one attested inference per document → a reduce
inference → a 0–1000 credit score**, and the **attestation hash is committed onchain** as the
credential's anchor. This replaces a price feed with a far harder problem — turning private,
real-world financials into a tamper-proof onchain signal — which is exactly what unlocks
undercollateralized lending.

- **Where:** `POST /api/lendsignal/score/submit` + `/finish`, `services/lendsignal/confidentialAi.ts`
  (model `gemma4`, base `confidential-ai-dev-preview.cldev.cloud`).
- **Onchain:** the attestation hash is written into the ENS credit record and bound into the
  EIP-712 `CreditAttestation` the lending vault verifies.
- **Verify:** run a credit check in-app — each document's attested inference id is shown and
  linkable; the score is persisted with its hashes (never the raw docs).

## 🪪 ENS — `*.kredito.eth` as the credit credential (ENSv2)

The credit identity **is** an **ENSv2 subname** (`<label>.kredito.eth`, an ERC-1155 name on the
Namechain/Sepolia stack) — so the name itself is the credential; no separate soulbound NFT. We
deployed a **split-authority resolver**: `kredito.status` + `lendsignal.attestation` are
**issuer-locked** (the business cannot forge "approved"), while profile records (name, avatar,
banner, website, socials) are **owner-editable**. Anyone can publicly verify a business at
`/identity/<label>` or look one up at `/verify` — a portable, verifiable reputation primitive.

- **Live on Sepolia:**
  - KreditoController `0xE498cbC0F0ED0b9059FEc2a7F1275834108915B0` (issuance authority)
  - KreditoResolver `0xE68F49F6256a2aF1702855dc62B82afF6Fd65F0E` (ENSIP-10, split-ACL)
  - subRegistry (ENSv2 UserRegistry) `0x2167d6DF85bC76f22b7f150220740444DC257AAf` under `kredito.eth`
- **Verify:** mint a name in-app, then open `/identity/<label>` (public card) and `/verify`.
- **Why it's novel:** ENS used as a **credit reputation layer** — issuer-attested status + owner
  profile on the same name, resolvable by anyone.

## 👛 Privy — gasless onboarding for non-crypto SMEs

Login is **email + Google only** (no external wallet). Every user gets an embedded wallet that
becomes an **ERC-4337 smart wallet with gas sponsorship** — so a small business with zero crypto
experience signs in and mints its onchain identity **without ever touching gas or a seed phrase**.
All user writes go through the sponsored smart wallet (`useSponsoredWrite`); message signing
(`useSmartWalletSign`) proves wallet control via ERC-1271/6492.

- **Where:** `services/web3/privyConfig.ts`, `hooks/scaffold-eth/useSmartWallet.ts`.
- **Verify:** sign in with an email — no wallet popup — then mint/customize your identity with no
  gas prompt.
